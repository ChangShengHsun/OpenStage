# GridStage 0.7.0

## Rehearsal tools

- **Understudies**: uncheck "Performing this run" to bench a dancer — they
  keep every position but disappear from the stage, exports and analysis
  until you check them back in.
- **Loop**: repeat the selected formation's walk-in + hold over and over —
  rehearse one transition without touching the timeline.
- **Walk-in (s)** field: edit a transition's length directly; only that
  formation moves.
- **Rehearsal review (AI)**: pause a calibrated reference video and click
  Review — per-dancer drift from the chart, whether the C-spot dancer is
  actually centered (select one dancer to designate the C-spot), and
  left–right symmetry.

## Editor & sharing

- Formation card colors on the timeline (e.g. one color per song section).
- Comment resolve: done notes collapse into a "Resolved" fold.
- Pen strokes and text pins now print in the walk-chart PDFs.
- **GIF export**: whole-show animated GIF (640px), rendered offline —
  perfect for the group chat.
- Full user manual in Traditional Chinese: docs/manual.md.
