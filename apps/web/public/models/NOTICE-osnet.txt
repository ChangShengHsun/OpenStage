osnet_ain_x1_0.onnx — person re-identification (appearance embeddings)

Source: OSNet-AIN from deep-person-reid (torchreid) by Kaiyang Zhou
(https://github.com/KaiyangZhou/deep-person-reid), MIT License.
Weights: osnet_ain_x1_0, the multi-source domain-generalization checkpoint
from the official model zoo, converted to ONNX on 2026-07-19, unmodified
architecture. (An earlier osnet_x0_25 export was replaced before release.)
